Image format

Image scaling

Typically, SVG images in webpages are quite small, but thanks to the properties of vector graphics, they can be scaled to any size without becoming pixelated or blurry. However, other image formats, such as PNG and JPEG, do not use vector graphics, and thus will lose detail as they are scaled up or down in size. A scaling factor is necessary to ensure that even SVG icons as small as 16px by 16px will still produce a PNG or JPEG image that is not blurry or overly pixelated.
